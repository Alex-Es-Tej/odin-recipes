Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Turkey sandwich loaded with veggies which makes it taste absolutely desirable.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 - 5 turkey slices
  • 2 tbsp of mayo or ranch
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 6 slices of cucumber
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Toast bread
  2. Rinse and slice tomato and cucumber
  3. Spread mayo/ranch on one side of the bread
  4. place tomatoes on top of the lettuce and cucumbers on top of the turkey
  5. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!
