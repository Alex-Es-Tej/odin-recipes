Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoon evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayanee pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  1. Preheat a grill for high heat
  2. Mix the ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, evaporated milk , Worcestershire sauce, cayanne pepper, and garlic in a large bowl using your hands. Form the mixture into 8 patties.
  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties until browned and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side.
